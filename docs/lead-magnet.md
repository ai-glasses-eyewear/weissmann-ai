Prepared 2026-07-23. Owner: Giovanna & Nicola. Status: ready to build (do **not**
duplicate the on-site missed-calls calculator — this magnet complements it).

Goal: capture visitors who are **interested but not ready to buy**, nurture them
truthfully, and move them to (a) call the live AI line **+41 56 539 18 67** and
(b) book a consultation via **/kontakt/**.

**Truth rules baked in:** no invented customers, testimonials, reviews, ratings,
certifications or statistics. The magnet contains **zero numeric claims** — the
only numbers are the visitor's own (via the existing calculator) and our own
published prices/facts (Swiss/EU data, DE/EN/FR/IT, human handover, WhatsApp/
email summary, no setup fee, live demo line).

## 1. Recommendation — the single best magnet

**The AI Phone Assistant Buyer's Checklist** — a 1-page (2-side) PDF: *13
questions that separate a phone AI that wins you customers from one that quietly
loses them*, each with "what a strong answer looks like," a simple score, and a
3-minute live test.

### Why this one (not the ROI worksheet)

- **No cannibalisation.** The site already has an interactive missed-calls ROI
  calculator (`MissedCallsCalculator.astro`). A downloadable ROI worksheet would
  repeat it and add friction. The checklist covers the *next* question a visitor
  asks after "this costs me money" → "how do I choose the right one?"
- **Right stage.** Not-ready buyers are **evaluating**, not calculating. A
  checklist is the classic evaluation-stage magnet: high perceived value, low
  effort, evergreen.
- **Covert qualification.** Every "strong answer" maps to a **real, published
  Weissmann capability** (Swiss/EU data, native DE/FR/IT/EN, human handover,
  calendar booking, WhatsApp/email summary, transparent CHF pricing with no setup
  fee, dedicated line, and a *public* live demo line). The visitor scores the
  market and Weissmann quietly scores full marks — without us disparaging anyone
  or inventing anything.
- **Trust-first, Swiss tone.** Vendor-neutral, advisory, honest — matches the
  site voice ("ehrlich, konkret und ohne Verkaufsdruck").
- **Feeds the demo naturally.** The last checkbox is "Can you call the real AI
  yourself before you buy?" → the CTA *is* the demo line.

## 8. How it feeds booking a demo (the conversion)

The magnet is deliberately built so **every exit leads to the demo**, and the
"demo" is frictionless because it's a phone number the visitor can call alone:

1. **Inside the PDF:** check 13 + "Test one in 3 minutes" + "Next step" all point
   to **call +41 56 539 18 67** and **book at /kontakt/**.
2. **Thank-you page:** primary buttons = "Call the live AI" and "Book a
   consultation." This is the first and warmest demo prompt.
3. **Email 1 & 2:** drive the self-serve demo (call the live line) — the lowest
   commitment, highest "wow" step.
4. **Email 3:** converts to a **booked consultation** (the human demo) via
   `/kontakt/`, which already runs the 5-touch CRM sequence in the growth
   playbook.
5. **Definition of success:** `call_ai_live` from a checklist source, and
   `generate_lead` / `book_consultation` on `/kontakt/` attributable to the
   nurture. Import both as Google Ads conversions (they already exist in GA4).

The live phone line **is** the demo — so "book a demo" carries almost no
friction, which is exactly what a not-ready audience needs.
