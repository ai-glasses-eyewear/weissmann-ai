# Weissmann AI Academy — Master TOPIC-CLUSTERS Blueprint

**Scope:** 9 content clusters · 1 pillar + full spoke set each · four-language parity (DE default · EN · IT · FR)
**Demand is expressed strictly in tiers** (niche · low · medium · high · very-high) — never as invented volumes.
**Difficulty tiers:** low · medium · high. **Commercial intent:** low · medium · high.
**Governing principles:** answer-first GEO build · Swiss/DACH lens (revDSG/nFADP, EU AI Act, CHF, data sovereignty) · named-author E-E-A-T (Giovanna Carpi, Nicola Moessner) · The Weissmann AI Method (Discover → Analyse → Design → Build → Deploy → Measure → Optimize) · no fabricated prices, stats, testimonials or legal claims.

**Build-order snapshot (by priority):** Clusters 2, 3, 6, 7, 8 (priority 9) lead, followed by Clusters 1, 4, 5, 9 (priority 8). Within each, ship the pillar + glossary + lowest-difficulty/highest-commercial spokes first.
